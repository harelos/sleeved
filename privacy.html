Privacy Policy

Sleeved — cash-envelope budgeting · Last updated July 22, 2026

Sleeved is built by TigerBrands Global to be private by default. It works fully offline, needs no account, shows no ads, and keeps what you track on your own phone. This policy explains, in plain language, exactly what that means.

The short version

  • We do not collect, upload, sell or share your personal data.
  • Everything you enter stays in local storage on your device.
  • There are no accounts, no logins, no advertising and no third-party analytics or tracking.

What Sleeved stores

Sleeved stores the envelopes you create, their names, amounts and balances directly on your device. This information never leaves your phone through Sleeved. If you uninstall the app or clear its data, it is permanently deleted.

Location & permissions

Sleeved does not request your location and does not use location services.

Purchases

Sleeved offers an optional paid subscription or upgrade. Payments are processed entirely by Google Play under Google's own terms and privacy policy. We never see or store your card number or billing details — we only receive confirmation from Google Play that an entitlement is active so the app can unlock its paid features.

Data sharing

We do not share your information with anyone, because we do not collect it in the first place. The only outbound connection the app makes is to Google Play for purchases. No advertising, marketing or analytics services are used.

Children's privacy

Sleeved is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

Security

Because your data is stored locally and not transmitted to us, it stays under your control and your device's own protections. We hold no servers containing your personal data.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and post the revised version at this same address.
